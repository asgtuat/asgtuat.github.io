Welcome to Advanced Systems Group!

Advanced Systems Group (ASG) at TUAT performs a broad range of systems software research. Of particular interest are systems software (e.g., operating systems, hypervisors, databases, etc.) design and implementation for dependable computing, big data processing, and cloud computing. ASG investigates pragmatic solutions applicable to real-world software consisting of millions of lines of code and brand-new hardware devices.
